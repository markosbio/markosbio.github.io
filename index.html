About Us

Markos Bio is an early-stage biotechnology venture exploring biocatalytic and bio-based approaches to sustainability and research-driven innovation.

We are currently in the research and concept-development phase, identifying scalable biological pathways that support circular bioeconomy models and impact-driven science in Africa.
